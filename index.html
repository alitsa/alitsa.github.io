Process

  1. Understand business goal
    • Internal conversation
  2. Identify target customer
    • Internal conversation
    • Data analytics, e.g. TAM
  3. Develop empathy; identify painpoints
    • Customer interviews
    • Experience mapping
  4. Brainstorm solutions
    • Team workshop
  5. Narrow in on a solution that serves the business goal
    • Internal conversation
  6. Test the value of that solution to customers
    • Concept mockups
    • Customer feedback
  7. Test the usability of the solution's design
    • High fidelity prototype
    • Usability tests
  8. Support the team as they work toward that solution incrementally
    • UI QA
    • Custmer feedback
    • KPI measurement
